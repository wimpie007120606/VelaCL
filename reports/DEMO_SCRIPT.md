# Two-minute demo script

1. Show `configs/base.yaml` and regenerate the versioned stream with `make data`.
2. Open the Stream Monitor and point to the real dataset hash and language breakdown.
3. Compare the four measured methods and explain one row of the forgetting heatmap.
4. Open Active Learning, inspect uncertainty/diversity and accept or correct one queued item.
5. Open Registry, show the quality gate and champion checkpoint lineage.
6. Submit English and isiZulu batches through `/docs`, then show confidence, warnings, and `/metrics`.
7. End on limitations: fixture scale, no external validity, no measured distributed/safety/quantization claims yet.
